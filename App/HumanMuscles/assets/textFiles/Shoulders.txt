	Shoulders:

The shoulders are a complex joint composed of three
major muscles: the deltoids, rotator cuff, and
trapezius. They are responsible for a wide range
of upper body movements, including overhead pressing,
rowing, and pulling. The size and definition of the
shoulders are often targeted by those who wish to
enhance the appearance of their upper body and
improve their athletic performance.

	Exercises for shoulders:

Military press

Dumbbell lateral raise

Rear delt flyes

Barbell upright row
