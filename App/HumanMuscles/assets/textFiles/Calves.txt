	Calves:

The calves are a group of muscles located in the
lower leg that play a significant role in walking,
running, and jumping. They are often targeted in
strength training exercises to improve lower body
strength and athletic performance. The size and
definition of the calves are also important for
those who wish to enhance the appearance of their
legs.

	Exercises for calves:

Calf raises

Jumping rope

Box jumps

Seated calf raises

and other...
