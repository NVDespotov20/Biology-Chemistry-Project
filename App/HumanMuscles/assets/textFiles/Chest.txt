	Chest:

The chest, also known as the pectoral muscles,
is a large muscle group located in the front of
the upper body. They play a significant role in
pushing movements, such as the bench press, as
well as posture and breathing. The size and
definition of the chest are often targeted by
those who wish to enhance the appearance of
their upper body and overall muscular development.

	Exercises for chest:

Bench press

Push-ups

Dumbbell flyes

Incline bench press
