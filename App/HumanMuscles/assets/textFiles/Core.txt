	Core:

The core is a group of muscles located in the
midsection of the body, including the abdominals,
obliques, and lower back. They play a significant
role in overall stability and posture, as well
as movements such as twisting and bending. The
size and definition of the core are often targeted
by those who wish to improve their overall fitness
and athletic performance.

	Exercises for core:

Planks

Crunches

Russian twists

Hanging leg raises

and other...
