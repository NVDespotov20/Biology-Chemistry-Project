	Traps:

The trapezius muscle, commonly known as the traps,
is a large diamond-shaped muscle located in the
upper back and neck. It plays a key role in
shoulder movement and stability, as well as
posture and neck movement. The size and definition
of the traps are often targeted by those who wish
to enhance the appearance of their upper body
and improve their athletic performance.

	Exercises for traps:

Barbell shrugs

Dumbbell shrugs

Farmer's walks

Upright rows
