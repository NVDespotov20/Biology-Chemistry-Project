	Traps:
The trapezius muscle, commonly
known as the traps, is a large
diamond-shaped muscle located in
the upper back and neck. It plays
a key role in shoulder movement
and stability, as well as posture
and neck movement.

	Exercises for traps:
Barbell shrugs,
Dumbbell shrugs,
Farmer's walks,
Upright rows,
