	Forearms:

The forearms are a group of muscles located in
the lower arm that play a significant role in
grip strength and forearm stability. They are
often targeted in strength training exercises
to improve grip strength and overall upper body
strength. The size and definition of the
forearms are also important for those who wish
to enhance the appearance of their arms.

	Exercises for forearms:

Wrist curls

Reverse wrist curls

Farmer's walks

Hammer curls

and other...
