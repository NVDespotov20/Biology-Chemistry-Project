	Glutes:

The glutes, also known as the buttocks, are a
group of three muscles located in the back of
the hip. They play a vital role in lower body
movements, such as walking, running, and
jumping. The size and definition of the glutes
are often targeted by those who wish to improve
the appearance of their lower body and overall
athletic performance.

	Exercises for glutes:

Squats

Deadlifts

Hip thrusts

Lunges
