	Triceps:

The triceps muscle is a three-headed muscle located
in the back of the upper arm that helps to extend
the elbow. It plays a significant role in pushing
movements, such as push-ups and bench presses.
The size and definition of the triceps are often
targeted by those who wish to enhance the appearance
of their upper arms and overall muscular development.

	Exercises for triceps:

Triceps pushdowns

Dips

Close-grip bench press

Overhead triceps extensions
