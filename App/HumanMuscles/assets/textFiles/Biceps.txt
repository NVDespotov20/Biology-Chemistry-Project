	Biceps
The biceps muscle is a 
two-headed muscle located 
in the upper arm that helps
 to flex the elbow and 
rotate the forearm. The size
 and definition of 
the biceps are often considered
 a sign of physical strength
 and fitness, and are sought
 after by many people who 
engage in resistance training.


	Exercises for biceps:
Biceps curls
Hammer curls
