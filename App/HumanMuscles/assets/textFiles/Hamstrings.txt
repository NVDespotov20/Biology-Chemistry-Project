	Hamstrings:
The hamstrings are a group
of three muscles located
in the back of the thigh that
play a vital role in leg
movement and stability. They
are often targeted in strength
training exercises to improve
lower body strength and
athletic performance.

	Exercises for hamstrings:
Deadlifts,
Romanian deadlifts,
Lying leg curls,
Stiff-legged deadlifts,
and other...
