	Hamstrings:

The hamstrings are a group of three muscles located
in the back of the thigh that play a vital role
in leg movement and stability. They are often
targeted in strength training exercises to improve
lower body strength and athletic performance.
The size and definition of the hamstrings are
also important for those who wish to improve the
appearance of their legs.

	Exercises for hamstrings:

Deadlifts

Romanian deadlifts

Lying leg curls

Stiff-legged deadlifts

and other...
